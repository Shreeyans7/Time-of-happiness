If anyone ask me what is the golden period of our life? I will clearly say, "The Student's Life". It is the happiest period of our life. In this span of time students are not affected by worries, tension stress etc.

It is the time when the students prepare themselves for their future life or career. This time will decide the future of any student. So, the students should not waste their time in unnecessary things because time is the most valuable and expensive thing in our life.

It is the base of entire life. If anyone wants to become a scientist he/she has to curious about new things since childhood. He/she should gather knowledge either from the parents, teachers or from the internet.

In this span of time, most of the students spend their time in unusual things like Social Media. It has become like a cancer disease to the students. Students might think that just like everything easily present on the screen with one click, they will also get everything easily in their life. Due to this most of the students get frustrated in their real life situations. It is the responsibility of the parents to make their child realize that everything is not easily present in our life, we have to do hard work to achieve the things that we really want in our life.
